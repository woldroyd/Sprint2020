Day 1

The first day will be mostly talks with an unconference sessions and project pitches

9:00 am PDT - Talk by Phil Marshall (Rubin Observatory/SLAC): Rubin Observatory + LSST Overview + Construction Update


9:30 am PDT - Talk by Lynne Jones (UW/Rubin Observatory): LSST Feature Based Scheduler, LSST Cadence Decisions Overview, and Solar System Metrics


10:00 am PDT - Talk by Mario Juric and Siegfried Eggl (UW): Overview of LSST Solar System Processing and Update on LSST Solar System Data Products


10:30 am PDT - Tutorial by Siegfried Eggl (UW), Sam Cornwall (UW) and Nate Lust (Princeton): The Simulated LSST Solar System Products database


11:00 am PDT - Break


11:30 am PDT - Talk by Leanne Guy (Rubin Observatory): Year 1 Template Generation and Year 1 Operations


12:00 pm PDT - Pitch projects for working groups


12:30 pm PDT - Unconference sessions


1:00 pm PDT - Sign up for working groups before the start the next day and vote on unconference sessions for Day 2
